English Country Dance

On the third or Fourth Friday, the Lexington English Country Dancers host a dance with local callers and musicians in Studio 3A, ArtsPlace. There is a lesson for beginners preceeding the dance. Cost: $5 ($2 for full-time students). Children 15 and under admitted free if accompanied by a responsible adult. For more information contact Terry at terryterry@windstream.net.
