Tango Practica

Dates: Every Wednesday
Time: 8:00 - 10:00 pm.
Place: Studio 3B, ArtsPlace
Instructors: None
Cost: $5 per person. Free to first timers.

Work Sessions give you a chance to work on your technique with your partner in a studio environment. Ask for feedback from fellow dancers, or just do your own thing. There is no lesson.
